[[Education]]
The education major prepares students to teach in the future. These majors can specialize in a specific subject as well.
The education major is very broad, and people can specialize in core subjects such as math, science, history, and english, and they can also specialize in fine arts. As well as teaching, those with an education major can also become school administrators, counselors, and can move up the education system ladder to become principals, district administrators, or even superintendents.
Average Entry level Salary: $43,000
