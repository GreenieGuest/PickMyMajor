[[Biology]]
Biology majors study living organisms, such as their function, evolution, interactions with the environment and each other, and more. Biology majors also study other sciences like chemistry, physics, and more. 
With a biology degree, people can go through many diverse pathways, such as research, healthcare, conservation, education, and more. Some careers for this major include biotechnologist, research scientist, microbiologist, physician, zoologist, pharmacist, and more.
Average Entry level Salary: Varies greatly, but is around $57,900.
