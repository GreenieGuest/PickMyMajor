[[Psychology]]
Psychology majors study the human mind and behavior. More specifically, psychology majors focus on human emotions, thoughts, and actions, and WHY humans feel, think, or do these factors. 
Some careers you can pursue with this major include clinical psychology, therapy, social work, counseling, and specialized psychology, such as forensic psychologist, industrial psychologist, rehabilitation psychologist, and more. 
Average Entry level Salary: Varies greatly, but can range from $45,000 to $88,000.
