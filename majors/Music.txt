[[Music]]
The music arts major focuses on the study of music, more specifically performance, composition, theory, history, and more. Music majors are similar to theater arts majors, where they have to perform to an audience. 
Some careers include music production, music artist/performer, sound engineering, music therapy, music coaching, music teacher, and more.
Average Entry level Salary: Varies greatly due to many factors including what specific job you have, but the average salary is $29,013.
