[[Accounting]]
Accounting majors focus on the study of creating and maintaining financial systems. Accounting majors more specifically explore financial statements, budgeting, tax law, and cost analysis, as well as gaining skills in organization, attention to detail, and time management.
Some careers you can pursue with this major include accountant(tax, management, etc), auditor, tax preparer, bookkeeper, financial analyst, financial planner, CFO, etc.
Average Entry level Salary: $54,749
